Caryopsis

Caryopsis is a semi-casual puzzle game in which you try to optimize a corn genetic mutation process: you have to recreate a specific corn cob by modifying the genes of the plants you have with as few modificaions as possible. It was made for the in-person Scientific Game Jam de Lyon 2024. The jam's goal is to create a game based on a scietific master's thesis. We had 24h to find a team (comprised of 2 game developpers, 2 artists, 1 game designer, 1 sound designer, and 1 scientist), debate on how to transform the scientist's thesis into a game, and create it.
