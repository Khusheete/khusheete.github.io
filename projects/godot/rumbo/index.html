Rumbo

By Higgaion Games

I made this game with 13 people from Higgaion Games, a small community of game developpers. We made it in 14 days for the Adventure Game Jam. However, we decided on a twist: we were not able to communicate during the jam, and we only had 24 hours each to work on the game.

In the game, you play as Rumbo, a character that arrives on an island overrun by monsters. Fighting them will require you to hit quick time events that determine if your attacks are successful or not.
