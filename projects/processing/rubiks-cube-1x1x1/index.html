1x1x1 Rubik's Cube

Here is a cube that rotates, thanks to the black mathematical magic that are quaternions. I heard of those 4D complex numbers in a 3blue1brown video, and had to play around with them. I find it facinating how this seemingly unrelated piece of math can be used for a very intuitive and elegant way to model rotations in 3D space.
