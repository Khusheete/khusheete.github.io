Sudoku Solver

A probably inefficient, but working threaded sudoku solver.


You can move the cursor with the arrow keys and write numbers in the grid. If you want to remove a number, type a zero. It is possible to save and load sudokus by pressing the 's' or 'o' keys, and pressing 'S' will take a screenshot of a solved sudoku. You can also stop the solving process by pressing 'n'.
