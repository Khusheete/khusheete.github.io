Battle

This is a battle simulator. It's the first project I made after I learned about classes. Though as you might see, I didn't know about inheritance or anything like that.

Controls:

  • 'p' to pause
  • 'a' to spawn units on the mouse cursor
  • 'c' to change unit type
  • 'r' to remove every unit from the battlefield
